February in the Garden

PREPARE & MAINTAIN
  • Water plants well before freezing temperatures occur. Make sure plants are mulched to protect them from cold.
  • Watch the forecast and protect garden plants with row cover if temperatures will be below 28 degrees F.
  • Purchase seed potatoes and pre-sprout for planting.
  • Add compost and fertilizer to planting beds.
  • Prune, feed and mulch roses.
  • Cut back and turn in cover crops to prepare beds for planting.
HARVESTING NOW
  • Arugula
  • Beets
  • Carrots
  • Chard
  • Kale
  • Leeks
  • Lettuce
SOW & PLANT INDOORS
  • Start tomatoes, peppers and eggplant seeds
  • Start containers of lettuce in sunny windows for indoor winter salads
SOW & PLANT OUTDOORS
  • Plant lettuce, cabbage, radishes, turnips and spinach seeds. Repeat every two weeks for regular harvests
  • Plant transplants of broccoli, cabbage, cauliflower, collards and mustard greens
  • Plant peas and protect them from birds with row covers
